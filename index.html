    I would like to work at a business that develops software for clients and helps them to run their business in the technological world. I have found something that seems to match my criteria. At ‘Alliance Software’ they are looking for a position for a “Senior Python & JavaScript Developer”.

  • What skills I need

    This job is looking for Python and JavaScript developers to help them create websites and software for clients. This job is appealing for me as I would enjoy all aspects of the business, whether it be signing on new clients to the business or developing software for them. It combines both aspects of business which I really like, the creative and business side. For this job I will need to gain the following skills:

    - Great coding skills: They are looking for someone who has years of experience with solving hard software problems. - Technical Self-Management: Be able to manage my own work well and be able to work well with tasks and my own planning. As a senior developer I will also need to learn how to set the pace and bring experience to the workplace. - Complex wed development experience: Be able to develop complex web applications and be very familiar with using Linux as well as Python and JavaScript frameworks.

  • What skills I have now

    Unfortunately, at this point in time my programming skills are rather average, however the skills I currently have are:

    - Leadership abilities:I am able to lead the team in the right direction and provide motivation as well as manage other employees inside the business.

    - Initiative: I can also bring my initiating attitude to the table by doing before I am told and knowing what is required from me rather than needing constant guidance, this can help me guide other employees.

    - Business-minded attitude: I have a very business orientated mindset and am able to sell myself or a service to other people. This will help a lot in this job as I am able to attract new clients and consolidate strong relationships with existing clients.
